Musicals

    "Hamilton: An American Musical" is a sung-through biographical musical about Alexander Hamilton, a key figure in the founding of the United States. The musical, written and composed by Lin-Manuel Miranda, chronicles Hamilton's rise from an immigrant orphan to a major political figure, highlighting his involvement in the Revolutionary War, his role in creating the U.S. financial system, and his eventual tragic death in a duel with Aaron Burr.
